About Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker has become one of the most popular idle games worldwide. As a Cookie Clicker player, you'll start as a humble cookie manufacturer, creating delicious cookies with just a click. The Cookie Clicker experience evolves as you progress, unlocking numerous automated facilities and upgrades to build your very own cookie empire.

How to Play Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker's core mechanic is beautifully simple: click the big cookie to produce cookies. The magic of Cookie Clicker reveals itself as you delve deeper, discovering incredible strategic depth and endless possibilities.

Your Cookie Clicker guide:

  • Getting Started: Begin your Cookie Clicker adventure by manually clicking the big cookie. Each Cookie Clicker click produces one cookie.
  • Automation: As your Cookie Clicker progress grows, purchase various facilities like cursors, grandmas, farms, and more for automatic production.
  • Upgrade System: Cookie Clicker's extensive upgrade options enhance both clicking efficiency and automatic production. Strategic choices are crucial.
  • Achievements: Cookie Clicker features numerous achievements waiting to be unlocked, providing both satisfaction and gameplay bonuses.
  • Golden Cookies: Special Cookie Clicker events appear periodically, offering powerful effects when clicked.
